MVPs

Minimum Viable Product Development Service

Diaxara offers MVP (Minimum viable product) development services, it is a simple way to verify business assumptions from custom software or test the demand of the product with minimum investment. If you are looking to launch your new MVP to meet the business goals, Diaxara offers MVP development services to satisfy your needs. Diaxara’s developer will assist you in designing the business goals using the existing resource. We provide in-depth analysis reports, showing you how MVP development fits your business plan. We transform your ideas into millions of users. We are ready to work on MVP for any small, medium, or large-scale business. With MVP development services, you can easily determine your products’ potential in the market.

Our MVP development service assists your MVP ideas and turns them into reality. Diaxara’s developer makes sure that your MVP ideas are tested first. Later, we assist you in launching the product with all its rich features and functionality, sorting out all the issues. The MVP development is an enhanced version of the prototype. The process involves strategy planning, idea generation, measuring, learning, analysis, testing, etc. One of the main reasons for MVP development service is to identify the market for specific products or services. Thus overall fulfilling the customer satisfaction with requirements.

How do We Build MVP in Diaxara?

Being a skilled MVP development company, we adhere to the conventional SDLC while enhancing it with the most helpful strategies and techniques for the specific project. Using this method, we can create efficient solutions that require the least amount of resources to gather the required data.

Information Gathering

Diaxara developer gathers complete information about the product and understands the main objective of the product. Checks for the user end validation. Take an in-depth study on competitor analysis and SWOT analysis before starting up the MVP development.

Technical Analysis

Once the complete study is done on the product scope. The developer work on how to face the technical challenges, feasibility analysis, etc. The developer at this stage analyses the right platform, tools, and technology to use.

Prototyping

Based on the technical analysis, an outline sketch is given. We provide a complete wireframe on the customer use case and functional flow. Thus you get a perfect visual of mockups as an overview.

Document Presentation

Features and functionality-based documentation is presented, which includes the customer’s use case, prototyping, designing, enhancement, technology, architectural designing, and layout decision.

MVP Development

Based on the document, requirement development is set up. Development is done with feedback taken from the entrepreneur or clients. Once the functionality development is done, the quality assurance team takes the testing process.

Launch

Now the software product is ready to launch. Deployment on production is carried out. The application is moved to live both on the web and mobile platforms. The enhancement of the software can be done on the later version.
