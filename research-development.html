Research & Development

IT Research & Development Services

Setting up or scaling in-house software engineering operations is often a costly and time-consuming thing. Especially when it's handled by people who don't already have experience and expertise in this domain. The challenge becomes even harder when you try to keep up with new development techniques, environments, platforms, and technology.

R&D, short for Research and Development, refers to a series of investigative activities that come beforehand the decision to improve existing products and procedures or to develop new solutions. Companies across all sectors and industries invest in R&D to help them improve their existing products and locate gaps in the market that can be filled with new solutions and thus experience corporate growth.

We at Dixara know the importance of R&D. As a company with significant experience in developing sophisticated software and IT solutions, we offer our extensive knowledge to our clients with the goal of helping them build product innovations and improvements that they can apply to widen their audience and reach desired business goals.

Why Choose Us

Technology Consulting

To gain maximum value from technology investments, we help our customers understand the exact value a specific piece of technology may bring to their business. We're talking here about things like improvement of staff productivity, cost savings, competitive edge, etc. Under this specific service, we also help customers successfully build tech adoption road maps (starting from license choice to implementation specification).

Digital Transformation Consulting

Under this service, we help customers figure out how to digitalize key business processes and increase the share of automated task fulfillment. We introduce a series of tools, processes, and tech solutions that improve the efficiency of their business across all departments.

IT Strategy Consulting

Under this service, our team of senior IT experts works with the customer to help align their IT assets with current and anticipated business goals. We assess the current state of your IT environment and provide strategic support to help you build a better, stronger, cost-effective, stable and scalable IT infrastructure.

How We Work

time & materials

In this model, you are charged for the total time spent by the project manager, developers and QA during the development stage according to the hourly rate.

fixed price

A contractual model that means that the project price is fixed at the outset for the known project scope and no actual work price will be taken into account.
